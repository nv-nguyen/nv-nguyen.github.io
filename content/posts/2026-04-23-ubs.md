[Rong Liu](https://rongliu-leo.github.io/), [Zhongpai Gao](https://www.gaozhongpai.com/), [Benjamin Planche](https://planche.me/), [Meida Chen](https://www.linkedin.com/in/meida-chen-938a265b/), **Van Nguyen Nguyen**, [Meng Zheng](https://meng-zheng.com/), [Anwesa Choudhuri](https://anwesachoudhuri.github.io/), [Terrence Chen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=S2BT6ogAAAAJ&hl=en), [Yue Wang](https://yuewang.xyz/), [Andrew Feng](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewfeng-ict/), [Ziyan Wu](https://wuziyan.com/)

**ICLR 2026**

A unified framework that generalizes 3D Gaussian Splatting to N-dimensional anisotropic Beta kernels for explicit radiance field rendering. Beta kernels naturally decompose scene properties into interpretable components (surface vs. texture, diffuse vs. specular, static vs. dynamic) without explicit supervision.
