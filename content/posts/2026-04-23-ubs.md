[Rong Liu](https://rongliu-leo.github.io/), [Zhongpai Gao](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=AFOCPkUAAAAJ), [Benjamin Planche](https://planche.me/), [Meida Chen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ii_J7K4AAAAJ), **Van Nguyen Nguyen**, [Meng Zheng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=TsgMRsUAAAAJ), [Anwesa Choudhuri](https://anwesachoudhuri.github.io/), [Terrence Chen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=PISXgwMAAAAJ), [Yue Wang](https://yuewang.xyz/), [Andrew Feng](https://ict.usc.edu/about-us/leadership/research-leadership/andrew-feng/), [Ziyan Wu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=1JDFQBUAAAAJ)

ICLR 2026

A unified framework that generalizes 3D Gaussian Splatting to N-dimensional anisotropic Beta kernels for explicit radiance field rendering. Beta kernels naturally decompose scene properties into interpretable components (surface vs. texture, diffuse vs. specular, static vs. dynamic) without explicit supervision.
